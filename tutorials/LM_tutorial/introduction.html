The provisoning of the VM installs SRILM if it is present in /vagrant/protected/srilm-1.7.1.tgz, as well as two data files (WSJtrain and WSJtest) if they are in the protected directory. These are licensed data/software, so they can't be directly provided. SRILM may be downloaded and installed for non-commerical use. WSJtrain/WSJtest are from the Wall Street Journal 0 corpus, licensed by the LDC.

The following resources may be useful to students in completing the tutorial:

  • SRI Language Modeling Toolkit main page
  • SRI Language Modeling Toolkit description paper (2002)
  • SRI Language Modeling Toolkit updates paper (2011)

     man ngram-count 
     man ngram
